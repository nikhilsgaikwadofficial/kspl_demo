# MOSIL LUBRICANTS - Employee Meeting Management System
## User Manual

---

## Purpose of the Application

The **MOSIL LUBRICANTS Meeting Management System** is an enterprise web application designed to streamline and manage all meeting-related activities for employees within the organization. This system serves as a centralized platform for organizing, tracking, and managing meetings efficiently.

### Primary Objectives:

1. **Centralized Meeting Management**
   - Provides a single platform for all employees to create, view, and manage meetings
   - Eliminates the need for manual scheduling and tracking through emails or spreadsheets
   - Ensures all meeting information is stored in one accessible location

2. **Real-Time Meeting Tracking**
   - Offers instant visibility into meeting statistics through the dashboard
   - Displays key performance indicators (KPIs) including:
     - Total Meetings
     - Upcoming Meetings
     - Ongoing Meetings
     - Completed Meetings
   - Enables employees to stay informed about their meeting schedule at a glance

3. **Enhanced Collaboration**
   - Facilitates easy participant management for meetings
   - Supports both online and physical meeting types
   - Allows employees to see who is organizing meetings and who is participating

4. **Meeting Lifecycle Management**
   - Tracks meetings through various stages: Upcoming, Ongoing, Pending, Completed, and Cancelled
   - Provides status badges for quick identification of meeting states
   - Enables employees to edit, reschedule, or cancel meetings as needed

5. **Time and Resource Optimization**
   - Calendar integration helps prevent scheduling conflicts
   - Meeting history tracking aids in planning and resource allocation
   - Reduces time spent on meeting coordination and follow-ups

6. **Task and Action Item Management**
   - Integrates with task management features ("My Tasks" tab)
   - Links action items to meetings for better follow-up
   - Supports review and approval workflows

### Key Benefits for Employees:

- **Efficiency**: Quick access to all meeting information in one place
- **Transparency**: Clear visibility of meeting schedules, participants, and status
- **Flexibility**: Easy creation, editing, and cancellation of meetings
- **Organization**: Structured view of meetings with filtering and pagination capabilities
- **Accountability**: Clear tracking of meeting organizers and participants
- **Accessibility**: Web-based platform accessible from any device with internet connectivity

### Who Can Use This System:

This application is designed for all employees of MOSIL LUBRICANTS who need to:
- Schedule and organize meetings
- View their meeting calendar
- Track meeting-related tasks
- Review and approve meeting-related items
- Manage their meeting participation

---

## System Overview

The application provides a user-friendly dashboard interface that displays:
- **Welcome Message**: Personalized greeting for the logged-in employee
- **Meeting Statistics**: Quick overview of meeting counts and status
- **Navigation Tabs**: Access to Meetings, Calendar, My Tasks, and Review sections
- **Meeting List**: Detailed table view with comprehensive meeting information
- **Action Buttons**: Quick access to create new meetings
